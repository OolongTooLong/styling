Who are X2X?

X2X is an entertainment technology company that partners with clients to enable creative flow across the production life-cycle with a suite of secure communication and content management solutions.

We design, develop, and manufacture software and hardware products for studios, broadcasters, and film and video production companies. Our solutions are represented by brands, PIX and CODEX.

For the Makers.

Our award-winning products for the media and entertainment industries include production solutions and high-performance recording and workflow tools in support of the leading camera vendors for feature, television, and commercial production.

At X2X we are passionate about inclusion, we want all employees to feel encouraged to be themselves and believe that this inspires creativity and innovation. X2X is an equal opportunities employer with a focus on inclusivity and equality for all.

X2X is a company that focuses on our people, harboring creativity and being a leading innovator in our Industry.

X2X Values

Ethics
We are uncompromising in our integrity, honesty, fairness, and transparency.

Security & Privacy
We are relentless in protecting our client’s information, privacy, and content.

Quality
We are passionate about excellence and doing our work the right way. Our reputation depends on our delivered value in the eyes of all clients, and our clients count on us to do our best work.

People
We inspire each other with important work, full of purpose, challenging development opportunities, and rewarding careers. We aspire to be the employer of choice in our industry.

Culture
We actively build a diverse, inclusive, and collaborative work environment where all views are welcomed, openness is encouraged, and teamwork and merit are cornerstones.

Relationships
We build positive, long term relationships with our clients, partners,vendors, and colleagues that are based on trust, respect, and collaboration.

Innovation
We develop, apply, and integrate with world-class technology by listening, learning, and seeking out the best ideas. We embrace responsible risk-taking and we learn from our mistakes.

Empowerment
We improve the quality of life of the clients we support. We empower our people and our clients to do their best work.

Autonomy
We are employee owned, self funded, self directed, and aspire to create an open and supportive environment that promotes creative problem solving and rewards challenging the status quo. We control our destiny.

The X2X Promise

At X2X we look to provide a package that helps you and your family, and to ensure you and your loved ones have everything you need to be happy and healthy, on your own terms. Every office has its own set of benefits built around the needs of our employees. Here’s an overview of some of the things that we offer:

Family
X2X supports employees with their family by offering an excellent work life balance. We are a flexible company and give you the right balance to look after yourself in order for you to look after your family.

Wellbeing and Health
As X2X sees the importance and wellbeing of our employees as our priority, we offer support and guidance on specific diagnoses as part of our employee assistance programme. This includes advice for anxiety and depression, guidance and advice on coping skills, mindfulness and relaxation, equality and diversity and emotional health articles.

Fresh fruit and snacks are provided in all of our offices.

Our managers have an ‘open door’ policy; a problem shared is a problem halved!

Culture and Fun
We are very proud of the culture we’ve built at X2X. We’re a friendly and supportive bunch who love to welcome new faces to the business and will always be up for a little after-work mixer. As we pride ourselves on being a communicative and dynamic company, you never feel like you're too far away from the right answer and someone is always there to lend you a hand. As part of our culture, we love to celebrate our successes from Christmas parties to mid-week lunches to after work drinks; there is usually something going on and everyone is welcome!

Diversity
We are passionate about inclusion. We want all employees to feel encouraged to be themselves and believe that this inspires creativity and innovation. We are building an inclusive culture and have created an Equity, Diversity and Inclusion team to allow any member of the company to participate and have their voice heard. Moving forward, we are looking to partner with organizations with a focus on diversity and will be continuing to bring in keynote speakers for workshops with the whole company on diversity and inclusion. X2X is an equal opportunities employer with a focus on inclusivity and equality for all.
